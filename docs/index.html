Welcome to CVNet’s documentation!

CVNet is a high-performance open-source library for training deep neural networks for visual recognition tasks, including classification, detection, and segmentation.

CVNet supports image and video understanding tools, including data loading, data transformations, novel data sampling methods, and implementations of several state-of-the-art networks with significantly better performance than the original publications.
